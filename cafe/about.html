Who We Are

Born from a Love of Coffee

Brew & Bean began as a humble dream in a small DHA apartment — two friends with a commercial espresso machine, a sack of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe, and an unshakeable belief that Lahore deserved world-class coffee.

That apartment experiment became a pop-up. The pop-up became a cafe. Today, we serve over 500 cups daily to regulars who've made us part of their morning ritual — and we couldn't be more proud.

Every bean we source, every pastry we bake, every cup we craft carries that same original conviction: that the details matter, that quality has no shortcut, and that a great cup of coffee can genuinely make someone's day better.
